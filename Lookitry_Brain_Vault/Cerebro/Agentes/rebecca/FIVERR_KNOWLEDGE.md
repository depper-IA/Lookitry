## 📋 HOW TO CREATE A GIG — Step by Step

### Step 1: Gig Title
- Max 80 characters
- Start with "I will..." (Fiverr convention)
- Include primary keyword (what buyer searches for)
- Example: "I will be your professional UGC female model for your brand"

### Step 2: Category Selection
**Primary Category:** Video & Animation → Video Testimonials
**Subcategory:** Online Video Testimonials

### Step 3: Pricing (3-Tier System)

| Package | Purpose | Price Rule |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Basic** | Entry point | Lowest price — use as anchor |
| **Standard** | Most popular ⭐ | Medium price — THIS IS WHAT YOU SELL |
| **Premium** | Full featured | Highest price — up to 5x Basic |

**Pricing Strategy for UGC Model:**
- Basic: $30 (1 video testimonial, 1 revision, 7 days delivery)
- Standard: $70 (1 video testimonial, 2 revisions, 5 days delivery) ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- Premium: $120 (2 videos, commercial rights, 3 revisions, 3 days delivery)

### Step 4: Gig Description (Formula)
```
[HOOK - 1 sentence]
Hi! I'm [Your Name] 👋 and I'm a [Your Niche] creator helping brands [benefit].

✅ What you get with this gig:
• [Feature 1]
• [Feature 2]  
• [Feature 3]

🎯 Who is this for:
• [Target customer 1]
• [Target customer 2]

📦 My process:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

Let's create something amazing together!
```

### Step 5: Requirements (Buyer Questions)
Ask only what's necessary:
- Script or talking points provided? (yes/no)
- Brand guidelines? (file upload optional)
- Video ratio preference? (9:16, 1:1, or 16:9)

### Step 6: Tags
- 5 tags max
- Mix of broad + specific
- Example: UGC, female model, brand video, testimonial, lifestyle, fashion, TikTok, Instagram Reels

### Step 7: Gallery (Videos/Images)
- 1 required video OR 1 required image
- Use a high-quality showcase video (15-30 seconds)
- Video should show you (UGC model in action)

### Step 8: FAQ (Optional but Recommended)
Common buyer questions:
1. "Do you offer revisions?" → Yes, see package details
2. "Can you promote my product?" → Absolutely! I specialize in authentic-feeling UGC
3. "What's your delivery time?" → See package options above

### Profile Optimization
- **Profile headline**: "UGC Creator | Fashion & Lifestyle Influencer | Lookitry Ambassador"
- **Profile description**: Human, warm, professional. Show personality.
- **Profile picture**: High-quality headshot (not selfie, not filtered)
- **English level**: Fluent (Rebecca is fluent — use this in profile)

### Gig Title Formulas That Convert
1. "I will be your female UGC model for TikTok & Instagram Reels"
2. "I will create authentic UGC product testimonial videos for your brand"
3. "I will be your professional spokesperson for your promotional video"

### Pricing Psychology
- **Anchor with Standard** — Standard at $70 becomes the "normal" price
- Buyers rarely choose Basic — Standard is where you close
- Premium exists for buyers who want more (commercial rights, multiple videos)
- Never go below $30 (Fiverr will flag it and it devalues your work)

### Response Rate Tips
- Respond within **3-8 minutes** (never before 2 min — looks bot-like)
- Always address buyer by name
- Ask 1 qualifying question
- Recommend Standard package with reasoning
- End with clear CTA

### Gig Images/Video Guidelines
- First image is the "thumbnail" — must be eye-catching
- Use 16:9 ratio for images (recommended 1280x720)
- For videos: 30-second showcase, not the full testimonial
- Show your face (buyers want to see the model)
- Behind-the-scenes content converts well for UGC

### Common Reasons Gigs Get Rejected
1. External links or email addresses in description
2. Price too low (under $30)
3. Stock photo or low quality gallery
4. Description too short (under 300 chars) or too vague
5. Title doesn't match category selected
6. Mentioning other platforms (TikTok, Instagram) — actually fine if used as examples of where content will be used
