## 💡 SLIDE 1: THE PROBLEM

### Fashion E-commerce Has a Crisis

**🚨 The Numbers:**
- **30-40%** of online fashion purchases are returned
- **$550B+ USD** lost annually to returns globally
- **64%** of shoppers hesitate to buy clothes online without trying them
- Returns cost retailers **15-30%** of the item's value

**😰 The Core Problem:**
> Customers can't try before they buy — leading to uncertainty, abandoned carts, and costly returns.

**🤯 For Brands:**
- Lost revenue from abandoned carts
- High return logistics costs
- Negative customer experience
- Difficulty showcasing products online

## ✅ SLIDE 2: THE SOLUTION

### Lookitry — Virtual Try-On with AI

**What We Do:**
Lookitry is a SaaS platform that lets fashion brands add an AI-powered virtual try-on widget to their online store in minutes. No app, no development work needed.

**How It Works:**
1. Customer selects a product
2. Uploads a selfie or chooses a model
3. AI generates a realistic image of them wearing the item
4. Customer makes a confident purchase decision

**🎯 Key Features:**
- ⚡ **5-Minute Integration** — Widget embeds in any e-commerce platform
- 👗 **All Categories** — Clothing, accessories, footwear, jewelry
- 🎨 **Realistic Results** — Powered by advanced AI/ML models
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard** — Track engagement and conversion
- 🌐 **Multi-language** — English + Spanish ready
- 🛒 **WooCommerce Plugin** — Direct WordPress integration
